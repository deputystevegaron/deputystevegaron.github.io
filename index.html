Furthering Education Through Scholarship

Deputy Steve Garon passed away unexpectedly in December 2009. A scholarship has been established in his memory to support and local students who plan to further their education in some form of public service.

Deputy Garon was a beloved member of the Bellingham Fire Department and an inspirational leader. His presence is sorely missed. However, with your participation and contribution, his legacy will live on through this scholarship.Â
